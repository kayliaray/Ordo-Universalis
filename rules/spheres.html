Spheres

Attributes make up spheres that shape every creature's physical and mental capabilities in the game.

First Sphere

The first sphere is the simplest of the spheres. All of the attributes mentioned below make up this sphere. Whenever there is a mention of the first sphere, it talks about each attribute collectively. "Sphere" is just a particular word for "Category."

Attributes

Attributes are tied directly to a creature's anatomy. Ordo Universalis relies on these attributes to quantify every being's physical and mental capabilities in the game. The following is a brief description of each attribute.

  • Constitution = physical cohesiveness
  • Strength = physical muscle
  • Dexterity = physical agility and flexibility
  • Awareness = connection to surroundings
  • Aptitude = ability to process data
  • Fortitude = a force of will

Every roll in the game relies on a mortal's attributes. Some rolls in the game will even rely on up to three of these attributes simultaneously. Players can put reward points into these attributes to improve them. However, there is a biological limit depending on the race of their character.

An attribute score of 6 is the human average. Adventurers and creatures will most likely have much higher scores than that. The biological limit of humans is 14 for each attribute. Different races will have varying attribute limits.

The Attribute Die Table indicates what die is rolled, derived from the relevant attribute score. A creature with a Constitution score of 10 will roll a d6 for any roll that requires a Constitution die.

Second Sphere

The second sphere gets a little more complicated. It's here where you will find general skills. Two attributes make one general skill. Whenever there is a mention of the second sphere, it talks about each general skill collectively.

General Skills

General skills are the result of a creature using two attributes in unison to overcome an event. Typically, these skills are learned without proper training and are the result of innate ability. The following is a brief description of each general skill.

  • Regimen = Strength + Dexterity
  • Reaction = Dexterity + Awareness
  • Brains = Awareness + Aptitude
  • Discipline = Aptitude + Fortitude
  • Toughness = Fortitude + Constitution
  • Brawn = Constitution + Strength

A creature with 10 Strength and 13 Dexterity will roll a d6 and a d10 on any general skill requiring a brawn check. The maximum values for general skills are their highest related attribute. Following the example above, this creature would have a maximum brawn of 13.

Third Sphere

The third sphere is the category for specializations. Three attributes combined make a specialization. Specializations quantify proficiencies in limited situations. The following is a brief description of each specialization.

  • Operations = Strength + Dexterity + Awareness
  • Technique = Dexterity + Awareness + Aptitude
  • Mental = Awareness + Aptitude + Fortitude
  • Leadership = Aptitude + Fortitude + Constitution
  • Brute = Fortitude + Constitution + Strength
  • Physical = Constitution + Strength + Dexterity
